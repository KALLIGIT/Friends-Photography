About us

Started by three friends with one camera

Friends Photography began in 2016 when three college friends pooled money for a single DSLR to shoot a cousin's wedding. Word travelled fast — the candid, joyful style we shot that day became our signature.

Today we're a team of twelve photographers, editors and drone operators working across Tamil Nadu, but the name stayed — because every couple, every family we shoot for, leaves feeling like they hired a friend, not a vendor.
